Not my question to answer (correctly directed at the human, not me) and not mine to act on (git commit of this scope, including binary sprite assets, is exactly the kind of decision Team Lead was right to escalate rather than delegate). **Watch for**: whether the human answers and whether the commit actually happens — if this sits unaddressed for many cycles, it's a real risk (any environment reset / fresh clone loses all migrated clip data, since only the working tree has it).
