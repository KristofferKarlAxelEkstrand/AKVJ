# Team Update: Inspiration from the Amiga demo scene

## Summary
I would really love for us to draw inspiration from the Amiga demo scene. There are a lot of interesting ideas there that could be valuable for this project, especially in terms of visual style and effects.

## Impact
Looking at these ideas could help us find fresh directions for effects, motion, and overall aesthetic expression.

## Action Needed
If anyone is interested, please feel free to research the demo scene and gather inspiration from it. For example, you could look online for effects ideas, techniques, and visual approaches that might translate well into our work.

## Notes
The goal is to be inspired by these ideas and maybe borrow concepts in a thoughtful way, rather than copy them directly.
