# Team Update: Progress and "Done" Status Concerns

## Summary
I am raising a concern about the current project momentum and task tracking. Specifically, we are observing a lack of meaningful progress, and items are frequently being moved to the "Done" state when they do not appear to be fully completed or verified.

## Impact
This practice obscures the actual state of the project, hinders our ability to accurately track real progress, and risks incomplete work or bugs accumulating. It causes confusion regarding what features are actually ready for use.

## Action Needed
- Review the recent tasks that have been marked as done to verify their true completion status.
- Re-align on a strict definition of "Done" for tasks moving forward.
- Please provide an update on why progress has slowed and how we can unblock the workflow.

## Notes
- It's important that we only transition tasks to "Done" after full validation.
